<section>
  <h3>1. Basics & Syntax</h3>

  <h4>Comments</h4>
  <p>코드에 영향을 주지 않는 메모입니다.</p>
  <pre><code class="code-snippet">// 이 줄은 실행되지 않습니다.</code></pre>

  <h4>End of Statement</h4>
  <p>모든 명령문은 <code>ok?</code> 또는 <code>okay?</code>로 끝납니다.</p>
  <pre><code class="code-snippet">myVar is 10 ok?</code></pre>
</section>

<section>
  <h3>2. Variables & Data</h3>

  <h4>Declaration (<code>is</code>)</h4>
  <p>새 변수를 선언하고 값을 할당합니다.</p>
  <pre><code class="code-snippet">heroName is "에이든" ok?
mana is 100 ok?
isReady is truth ok?</code></pre>

  <h4>Assignment (<code>Set</code>)</h4>
  <p>이미 선언된 변수의 값을 변경합니다.</p>
  <pre><code class="code-snippet">Set mana to mana - 10 ok?</code></pre>

  <h4>Data Types</h4>
  <p><b>Number:</b> 10, 3.14<br><b>String:</b> "Hello, World!"<br><b>Boolean:</b> truth, lie</p>
</section>

<section>
  <h3>3. Expressions</h3>

  <h4>Arithmetic</h4>
  <p><code>+ - * /</code>와 <code>()</code>를 지원합니다. 우선순위는 <code>* /</code> → <code>+ -</code>, 단항 <code>-</code> 허용.</p>
  <pre><code class="code-snippet">a is 1 + 2 * (3 + 4) ok?
b is -5 + 10 / 2 ok?
Reveal: "a={| a |}, b={| b |}" ok?</code></pre>

  <h4>Magic Ink (<code>{|...|}</code>)</h4>
  <p>문자열 안에서 <b>표현식</b>을 보간할 수 있습니다.</p>
  <pre><code class="code-snippet">x is 3 ok?
y is 4 ok?
Reveal: "x+y={| x + y |}" ok?</code></pre>
</section>

<section>
  <h3>4. Control Flow</h3>

  <h4>When / Otherwise / Conclude</h4>
  <p>자연어 비교와 논리연산자를 지원합니다.</p>
  <ul>
    <li>비교: <code>is</code>, <code>is not</code>, <code>is greater than</code>, <code>is less than</code></li>
    <li>확장: <code>is at least</code> (≥), <code>is at most</code> (≤), <code>is between A and B</code> (A ≤ x ≤ B)</li>
    <li>논리: <code>and</code>, <code>or</code>, <code>not</code> (우선순위: <code>not</code> → <code>and</code> → <code>or</code>)</li>
    <li>괄호로 조건 그룹핑</li>
  </ul>
  <pre><code class="code-snippet">hp is 30 ok?
mana is 10 ok?
stunned is lie ok?
when hp is greater than 0 and mana is at least 10 and not stunned:
  Reveal: "공격 가능" ok?
otherwise:
  Reveal: "행동 불가" ok?
conclude

level is 12 ok?
when level is at least 10 and (level is at most 20 or level is 99):
  Reveal: "입장 조건 통과" ok?
otherwise:
  Reveal: "입장 불가" ok?
conclude

x is 7 ok?
when x is between 3 and 10:
  Reveal: "x는 구간 안(포함)입니다" ok?
otherwise:
  Reveal: "구간 밖" ok?
conclude</code></pre>
</section>

<section>
  <h3>5. Input / Output</h3>

  <h4>Reveal (Output)</h4>
  <p>콘솔에 텍스트나 값을 출력합니다.</p>
  <pre><code class="code-snippet">Reveal: "게임 시작!" ok?</code></pre>
</section>

<section>
  <h3>6. Errors</h3>
  <ul>
    <li>라인 번호 포함 에러(중첩 블록 포함)</li>
    <li>예약어 변수명 금지, 0으로 나눗셈 금지, 잘못된 구문 즉시 오류</li>
  </ul>
</section>
